NIVIN PAULY

He came to be known for playing the boy next door archetype following roles in the bilingual black-comedy thriller Neram (2013),
the sports-drama 1983 (2014), the romantic-comedy Ohm Shanthi Oshaana (2014),
the romantic-drama Bangalore Days (2014), the comedy road-thriller Oru Vadakkan Selfie (2015), and the coming-of-age romantic drama Premam (2015)
